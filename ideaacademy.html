Idea Academy

Apprenticeship and Accelerator Programs

Idea Validation

We sift through ideas, develop the right ideas, test these ideas using landing pages and analytics to refine the ideas. We use quantitative measures to define the potential of the idea.

Resources

For ideas that pass our validation phase, we provide the resources (e.g. seed funding, design and development team members, office infrastructure etc.) required to launch the product

Mentoring

Though out the process, we use our network of mentors to help idea teams to stay on the right track and pivot when required. We ensure that the product teams are using the 'Build-Measure-Learn' cycle

Traction

After the product is launched, typically in 60 - 90 days, we develop and refine the traction channels to ensure sufficient traction for the product to acquire and retain customers at a sustainable rate
