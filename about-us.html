About Us

Big Challenges become our Big Opportunities

Our Culture

Our founders and team members have been helping clients achieve their ambitious goals for over 20 years. We have come together as ThinkBridge, so we can collectively add dramatic depth and breadth to our problem solving abilities. Compounding that with our passion for making technology more human, our holistic approach will help you make your big product ideas a reality. We are your technology partners and that means only when you succeed, we succeed.

1
Autonomy

We believe in full autonomy and we trust our team to do the right thing. Our team members are self directed and accountable for delivering value to their clients every day. Our processes exist to keep us disciplined but flexible. We understand change is the only constant and we are adaptive by design.

2
Intrapreneurship

Our interaprenuership model allows and requires each product team to manage themselves and their deliverables as if they were running their own firm. Our flat organization structure is designed to nurture entrepreneurship which is also the career path for our team members.

3
Continuous Learning

We belive in the "Beginner's Mind" philosophy and continuously learning and sharing that knowledge is a key part of our culture. Learning includes technical skills, life skills and interest based skills.

4
Purpose Driven

The goal for each team member is to deliver value driven by purpose. Each team member defines their purpose and how that purpose will benefit them, their clients and their co-workers. Team goals are defined around these purpose statements.
